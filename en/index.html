Sustainable Art

At Pulse and Art Studio, we believe in the power of watercolors, not just for their beauty but also for their sustainability. Unlike oil or acrylic paints, watercolor is water-based and contains fewer harmful chemicals, making it a safer choice for both the artist—especially for children—and the environment. We also buy our watercolor supplies from sustainable sources. Additionally, when properly protected, watercolor paintings can last for many years without losing their vibrancy. By choosing watercolor, you are supporting a more eco-friendly, lasting, and health-conscious approach to art.
